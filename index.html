About Me

Hi! I'm Hudson Tinch, a robotics enthusiast and maker with a passion for turning ideas into tangible creations.

As the captain of the HP robotics team, I combined my love for hands-on building with advanced technology, leading my team in challenging competitions and innovative projects.

Beyond robotics, I have a deep enthusiasm for creating physical objects, particularly through 3D printing. From prototyping intricate designs to building functional components, I thrive on the process of transforming digital concepts into real-world objects. For me, the intersection of engineering and creativity is where the magic happens, and I'm constantly exploring new ways to bring ideas to life.
